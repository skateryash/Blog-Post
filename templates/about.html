About Me

This is what I do.

Hey there, I'm Yash Chaudhary, a college student studying Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. I'm also a blogger and entrepreneur, and I'm excited to share my journey with you through my blog.

I started this blog as a way to connect with others who are going through similar experiences. As someone who experiences a wide range of emotions on a daily basis, I understand how overwhelming it can be when you feel like you don't have anyone to talk to. That's why I'm creating a space where we can share our stories and connect with each other.

On my blog, you'll find posts about my daily life lessons, failures, experiences, emotions, and my goals. I believe that by documenting my journey, I can help others who may be going through similar struggles.

Before pursuing my degree, I started my first business at the age of 16, and since then, I've been involved in multiple other businesses. I've even been part of three different Guinness World Records! Through my experiences as an entrepreneur and student, I've gained a lot of valuable life lessons that I hope to share with you through my writing.

My writing style is conversational and relatable. I want you to feel like you're talking to a friend when you read my posts. My target audience is college students who want to succeed in life despite the emotional trauma and challenges that come with it. My goal is to help you gain clarity on how to tackle problems in your life and move forward.

If you have any questions or want to share your own story with me, feel free to send me a message through the "contact" section on my website. I can't wait to connect with you and share our journey together.
